About Me

Born and grew up in Shandong, I went to Chongqing in 2011 when it came time for college. After graduation, I relocated to Delaware in the United States for postgrad studies and work. In 2022, I returned to China and joined the faculty at XJTLU in Suzhou. My research interests include blockchain, cryptography, distributed AI, and smart transportation.
