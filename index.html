About Me

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Delaware, supervised by Dr. Mark Nejad. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Delaware in 2017 and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the Chongqing University in 2015.

My research interests are the security and privacy in blockchain technology and its inspired applications such as connected and autonomous vehicular networks, electronic health records and federated learning.
