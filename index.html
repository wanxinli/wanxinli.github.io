About Me

Hello! I am currently an assistant professor in the Department of Communications and Networking at XJTLU. My research areas include blockchain technology, security & privacy, and federated learning. Before XJTLU, I received my doctoral & master's degrees from the University of Delaware in the US and my bachelor's degree from Chongqing University in China. I also hold a postgraduate certificate in Education from the University of Liverpool and a fellowship from the Higher Education Academy in the UK. In my free hours, I enjoy hip-hop music and a few vintage collections.
