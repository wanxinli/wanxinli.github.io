About Me

Born and grew up in Shandong, China, I went to Chongqing when it came time for college. I then moved to Delaware, USA for postgrad studies. After that, I returned to China and joined the faculty at XJTLU in Suzhou. I love teaching, and my research interests include blockchain, cryptography and smart transportation. I'm also a basketball and hip-hop fan since junior high in the 2000s.
